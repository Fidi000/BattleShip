Go to the Login Page, found here: login

Logging in

If you have already created an account, type in your username and password into the boxes.
Next, click the blue 'Login' button and you will be taken to the game page.

Signing up

If you are creating a new account, just hit the red 'Sign Up' button and your account will automatically be created.
If someone already has the username you entered, try a different username.

Go to the Game Page, found here: Play

Once on the page, click the 3 bars in order to open up your ship list.

Once open, you are able to select the ship you want to place by clicking on 'place'.
If you want to flip the orientation of the ship, just click flip once you've selected a ship to place.

Once you've selected a ship, place it anywhere on the left board (Your board).
Make sure you place all your ships before you start the game.

In order to play a game, go to the page page found here: play, and place your ships first.

Once you have your ships placed, click 'READY'.

Once you've click ready, if you are going to invite your friend, type their username into the invited box.
then click start game.

If you are joining your friend, give them your username so that they can invite you to their game.

Go to the Game page and set up game in order to begin firing. Game Page

When it is your turn, a 'Fire' button will appear at the bottom center of the page.

Click the 'Fire' button and then click anywhere on the enemy board (right board)
to fire a torpedo to.

If you hit an enemy ship, an 'x' will appear in the box where you fired a missle.

If you there is no ship where you fired, a 'o' will appear where you fired a missle.
